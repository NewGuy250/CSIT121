What is this website for?

Hello, my name is Daniel. I created this website to show off the photos I've taken ever since I've gotten into photography. The camera I own is a Panasonic LUMIX G95. Not all the photos on this website are from my camera, it could also be my phone.
I drew inspiration to make this website from Instagram and Wix.
I got inspiration from those websites because they both are ways for people to share photos they take, whether it's through social media or a blog.
On this website you will mostly find photos of my travels, food, and cat named Ping Pong.
I hope you enjoy!

Some Facts About the Photos

  • For my camera I have two different types of lens. (12-60mm F3.5-5.6 Lens and 45-150mm F4.5-5.6)
