Soufflé Pancakes, I got these from a place called, Motto Cafe. This is one of the best desserts I've ever had. They are really fluffy, they aren't super sweet like other dishes, and the fruit compliments the pancakes well. 10/10

I got these loaded fries from some fried chicken bar in Japan. I don't remember the name of the place. These were really good, I sat at the bar where they cooked in front me, which only raised my excitement. 8/10

Beef Hot Pot, I got this from a chain called Tasty Pot. It's a huge portion size and has a fire underneath the bowl so it stays hot. I like how many different things it has, it gives the dish variety. 9/10

Pig Buns, I got them from a place called Ding Ding Dumplings House. These buns are stuffed with custard and we're really delicious. It taste like a slightly sweet eggy bun. These are a must get for me whenever I eat dim sum. 8/10

Creamy Udon, I got this from a place called Udon Muzigo. This place has very good udon, I've had many things from here. I would definitely come again. 9/10

Poke Bowl, I got this from a place near the beach called, Jus' Poke. This is some of the best raw tuna I've had. Each part of the bowl also compliments each other part well. 9/10
